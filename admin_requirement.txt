Core Admin Pages (8 Main Sections) 
1. Login & Team Login
Admin/team member login page (email, password, OTP)
Role-based access (e.g. Super Admin, Procurement, Packing, Delivery & Custom user access)

2. Dashboard (App & Website Overview)(Both hub & Multi store)
Key stats: Total orders, sales, inventory, users, delivery status
Custom graphs: Sales trend, order status breakdown, stock level alerts
Quick links: Pending orders, low stock

3. Order Management (Both hub & Multi store)
View/manage all orders (live, manual, sources: WhatsApp, Insta, FB)
Assign orders to delivery partners and track updates
Multi-store and hub order switching manual
Manual user/order creation if source is not app/web
Update order status logic and history (e.g. Received, Packed, Delivered)
Order Filtering & Search (Quick Search, Date Range Filter, Status Filter, Hub/Zone Filter, Payment Method & Delivery Slot )

4. Team & User Management (Both hub & multi store)
Manage all team IDs (packing, procurement, delivery, etc.)
Assign/track orders per team/delivery partner
View team performance & delivery history
User/customer management (view, edit, deactivate, details, address search & Google Maps)
Delivery Agent Management (Add,remove,deactivate, Verify, order assignment, rating & review, History ) 

5. Product & Stock Management (Both hub & multi store)
Add, update, delete products (edit price, info)
Manage labels, packing, procurement (cutting type report)
Product & Stock status per store (multi-store supported)
Bulk update tools

6. Report Center
Auto-generate customizable reports (daily, weekly, monthly)
Packing, procurement, labeling, printable labeling, stock reports	
Download/share options for admins/teams
Release and restore functionality for corrected reports

7. Marketing & Notifications
Coupon code/offers management, set validity/running times
Scratch card rewards management
Spinner wheel rewards management
Automated/manual push notifications and custom messages to users
Subscription and in-app currency updates
Google Maps with geo-pin for address & delivery

8. Support & Settings
Live chat with users
Release management (app updates, feature toggles)
Manage legal: Privacy & Terms (edit/view, shown on app footer)
Easy 3rd-party integration: Connect other platforms if needed
Contact us & admin help
Feature toggles (enable/disable features)
Delivery slot configuration


Features in Every Main Page
Clear sidebar navigation, breadcrumb trails, role-based quick filters, Date Range Filter
Search, pagination, and responsive layout for easy mobile/tablet admin
Export options for most tables/reports (CSV, PDF, Excel)
All key actions in 1-2 clicks (assign, update, delete)
 
Additional Built-In Tools
Spinner wheel rewards shown in order/update flows
Automated reports and alerts for future scalability (future-proofed widgets)
Google Maps integration (address entry, route planning for deliveries)
Audit logs for all critical changes (orders, team, stock, delivery)
Restore previous states (rollback management)
Multi-language support: English & Tamil languages
Predictive analytics: AI-powered demand forecasting (optional)
SEO friendly website
